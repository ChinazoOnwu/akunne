Who we are

Akunné is a celebration of strength, individuality, and the beauty of becoming. Akunné lives at the intersection of jewellery and personal development. When you wear Akunné, you're not just accessorizing. You’re aligning with the woman you’re becoming.

About Our Founder

Akunna Onwu

CEO & Founder

Akunna is a certified executive life coach, former lawyer, and the founder of Akunné. Her own journey has been one of courageous transformation—leaving behind a life that looked perfect on paper to pursue one grounded in truth, purpose, and freedom.

She understands the ache of living out of alignment, the strength it takes to shed old identities, and the beauty of rebuilding from within. Through her coaching and handcrafted jewellery, she holds space for women on that same path—those ready to remember who they are and rise into their power.Her work is not about perfection—it’s about presence, healing, and becoming.
